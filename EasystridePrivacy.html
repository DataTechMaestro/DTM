Privacy Disclosure

Effective Date: 06 July 2025

Welcome to EasyStride (“the App”). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any personal data you entrust to us. This Privacy Disclosure explains what information we collect, how it is processed entirely on your device, and the rights you have under applicable privacy laws such as the Connecticut Data Privacy Act (CTDPA), California Consumer Privacy Act as amended by the CPRA (CCPA/CPRA), and the European Union General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

1. Our Core Privacy Principles

  1. On‑Device Processing First – All activity data is processed locally on your device by default.
  2. No Unnecessary Collection – We only collect data needed to provide core functionality.
  3. No Sale or Sharing – We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for advertising or any other purpose.
  4. Transparency & Control – You have clear in‑app settings to export, delete, or turn off data collection features.

2. Information We Collect & Why

Category Examples Purpose Storage
Activity Data Steps, distance, workout sessions, custom goals Calculate progress and display metrics Stored securely in an encrypted local database on your device
Device Data Device model, OS version, language, time‑zone Ensure compatibility and optimize performance Stored locally; included in crash logs if you opt‑in
Diagnostics (Optional) Crash reports, anonymized performance metrics Identify bugs & improve stability Processed on‑device; you choose if summaries are shared
Note: No raw activity or personally identifying data ever leaves your device unless you explicitly export it.

3. How We Process Data

  • Real‑time Analytics – Movement and sensor data are analysed by on‑device algorithms to compute streaks, caloric burn, and other statistics.
  • Personalisation – Your past activity is referenced locally to tailor motivational prompts.
  • Optional Cloud Back‑Up – If you enable iCloud/Google Drive backup through your OS, encrypted files may be synced per your platform provider’s terms.

4. Permissions We Request

Permission Reason Can You Decline?
Motion & Fitness / Physical Activity Access accelerometer & gyroscope for step counting Yes, but most features will not work
Notifications Send motivational reminders Yes – manage in system settings
File Storage Export or back‑up your data in .CSV / .JSON Yes

We never request Bluetooth, Location, Contacts, or Advertising IDs.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

  • All data remains on your device until you delete the App or use Settings → Delete My Data.
  • Back‑ups you create remain under your control via your chosen storage provider.

6. Security Measures

  • Encryption‑at‑Rest: Activity database is encrypted using AES‑256.
  • Encryption‑in‑Transit: If you opt‑in to send diagnostics, they are transmitted over TLS 1.3.
  • Secure Coding: We follow OWASP Mobile Security guidelines and perform regular penetration testing.

7. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to:

  • Access – Obtain a copy of your data.
  • Portability – Export data in a machine‑readable format.
  • Correction – Fix inaccuracies.
  • Deletion – Permanently erase data.
  • Opt‑Out – Object to processing or sharing (CTDPA/CCPA) – Already default in this App.

Request exercise: Use Settings → Privacy → Request or email datatechmaestro@gmail.com. We will respond within 45 days.

8. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from them. If you believe we have collected data from a minor, please contact us for deletion.

9. Changes to This Disclosure

We may update this Privacy Disclosure to reflect legal or operational changes. We will notify you in‑app and update the “Effective Date” above.
